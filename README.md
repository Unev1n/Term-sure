# 📖 About

**Term Sure** is an AI-powered browser extension designed to make **Terms & Conditions, Privacy Policies, Cookie Policies, and other legal agreements easy to understand.**

Instead of forcing users to read lengthy legal documents filled with complex legal language, Term Sure automatically detects legal agreements, analyzes them using Artificial Intelligence, and presents clear, actionable insights before users give their consent.

Our mission is simple:

> **Transform digital consent from a routine click into an informed decision.**

---

# ❓ The Problem

Every day, millions of internet users accept Terms & Conditions without reading them.

Why?

- 📄 Legal documents are too long.
- ⚖️ Legal language is difficult to understand.
- ⏱️ Reading every policy is time-consuming.
- 🔒 Users often don't know what data they are sharing.
- ⚠️ Important clauses are hidden within lengthy paragraphs.

According to multiple studies:

- **91%** of users accept Terms & Conditions without reading them.
- Only **9%** actually read them before clicking **"Agree."**

---

# 💡 Our Solution

Term Sure acts as an intelligent legal assistant directly inside your browser.

Whenever a website presents a legal agreement, the extension:

✅ Detects it automatically

✅ Retrieves publicly available legal documents

✅ Uses AI to analyze them

✅ Highlights important clauses

✅ Explains legal language in simple English

✅ Shows privacy insights

✅ Generates a Trust Score

This allows users to make informed decisions before accepting digital agreements.

# 🔒 Privacy

Term Sure is designed with user privacy in mind.

- Does not store browsing history.
- Does not collect passwords.
- Analyzes only publicly available legal documents.
- Secure communication with AI services.

---

# ⚠️ Current Limitations

- AI summaries are informational and not legal advice.
- Some websites restrict access to legal documents.
- Dynamic or region-specific policies may require additional processing.
- Trust Scores provide guidance and are not definitive legal assessments.
